# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 26, 2026*

TrackyFi ("the App") is a personal finance tracker built for India. This page explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Google Account
When you sign in with Google, the App receives your email address. This is used solely to associate your sync data with your Google account.

### Google Drive
If you enable sync, the App creates a single spreadsheet named **"TrackyFi Sync Data"** in your own Google Drive. This file is created under your account and is owned by you. TrackyFi does not store this data on any external server.

### Local Storage
All expense and wealth data is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted anywhere unless you explicitly trigger a sync.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect analytics or crash reports.
- We do not serve ads.
- We do not share your data with any third party.
- We do not store your data on our servers.

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
- **Local data:** Uninstall the App or clear the App's storage from Android Settings.
- **Drive data:** Delete the "TrackyFi Sync Data" spreadsheet from your Google Drive.
- **Account access:** Revoke TrackyFi's access from your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
