# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 26, 2026*

TrackyFi ("the App") is a personal finance tracker built for India. This page explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights over that data.

## Data We Collect

### Google Account
When you sign in with Google, the App receives your email address and basic profile information (name, profile photo). This is used solely to identify your account and associate your sync data with it.

### Google Drive
If you enable sync, the App creates a single spreadsheet named **"TrackyFi Sync Data"** in your own Google Drive. This file is created under your account and is owned by you. TrackyFi does not store this data on any external server.

### Local Storage
All expense and wealth data is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted anywhere unless you explicitly trigger a sync.

## Google API Scopes

TrackyFi requests the following Google account permissions:

| Scope | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| `drive.file` | Create and sync the "TrackyFi Sync Data" spreadsheet in your Drive |
| `userinfo.email` | Identify your account to associate sync data |
| `userinfo.profile` | Display your name and photo in the app |

The App only accesses the single spreadsheet it creates. It does not read, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect analytics or crash reports.
- We do not share your data with any third party.
- We do not store your data on our servers.

## Third-Party Services

TrackyFi uses **Google Sign-In** and **Google Drive** to provide optional sync functionality. When you use these features, Google's own privacy practices apply. Please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles your data.

## Data Security

All authentication is handled by Google's official Sign-In SDK — TrackyFi never sees or stores your Google password or OAuth tokens directly. Local data is stored in Android's sandboxed app storage, which is not accessible to other apps on your device.

## Children's Privacy

TrackyFi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this App, please contact us at **4gokul2000@gmail.com**. Since all data is stored on the user's own device and Google Drive — not on our servers — we will guide you through removing it using the steps in the Data Deletion section above.

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
- **Local data:** Uninstall the App or clear the App's storage from Android Settings.
- **Drive data:** Delete the "TrackyFi Sync Data" spreadsheet from your Google Drive.
- **Account access:** Revoke TrackyFi's access from your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
